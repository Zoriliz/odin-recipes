Philosopher's Sandwich

Description

Perfectly nutritionally balanced, this handheld dish is a busy Sharlayan researcher's best friend.

Ingredients

  • Dark rye bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Japanese Mayo

Steps

  1. Crack and scramble 2 eggs in a bowl
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and melt butter in the pan
  3. Pour egg mix into the mix, along with salt, pepper, and onion for taste
  4. Cook eggs fully, making sure to stir them constantly
  5. Place a piece of dark rye bread on a plate and add Japanese mayo
  6. Next stack the lettuce, cucumber, and eggs on the bread
  7. Lastly, top with another slice of dark rye
  8. Enjoy!
