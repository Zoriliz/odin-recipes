Archon Burger

Description

Concocted by the staff at the Last Stand, this sandwich is as nutritionally sound as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 0.25 lb ground chuck beef, (80/20)
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 burger buns
  • 1 slices medium cheddar cheese, (thick sliced)
  • 0.25 large tomato, sliced into rings
  • 2 leaves green leaf lettuce
  • 0.13 medium red onion, thinly sliced into rings
  • 0.13 cup dill pickle slices

Steps

  1. Slice, and prepare all of your burger toppings and sauces.
  2. Spread the cut-side of the bun with softened or melted butter and toast the cut side on a skillet or on the grill until golden brown. You can toast the bun ahead or in the last couple of minutes with the burgers on the grill.
  3. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  4. Divide ground beef into portions and form patties that are about 1/2" thick and 1" wider than the burger buns. Season patties generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Grill hamburger patties (indent-side-up) covered for 3-5 min each side on the grill. Flip burgers when juices are accumulating on top of the burger and you have a good sear.
  6. Apply cheese in the last 1-2 minutes of grilling then cover with the lid and cook until the internal temperature of beef reaches 160ËšF.
  7. Once patty is cooked, prepare buns by adding sauces of choice (mayo, ketchup, dijon mustard, etc.) and then stack the patty, lettuce, tomato, red onion, pickle slices, and then the top bun.
  8. Additionally you can pair with a side of choice such as fried or onion rings.
  9. Enjoy!
